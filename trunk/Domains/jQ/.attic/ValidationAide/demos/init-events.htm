Form Validation - Initialise Events

You can enable and disable the validator on click events:

More complex, says "hello" before the form validation kicks in:

$j(document).ready(function() {
  jQuery.validationAide.resetForm("#TestForm");
  $j("#SubmitButton").bind("click", function(){
    alert("Hello");
    jQuery("#TestForm").validationAideEnable();
  });
});
